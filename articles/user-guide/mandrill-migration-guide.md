In the wake of the changes at Mandrill, SparkPost has offered to take on any departing Mandrill users and to honor Mandrill’s pricing for those users. As a company, SparkPost would like to make the transition as seamless as possible. Please view our [Migration guide](https://www.sparkpost.com/mandrill-migration-guide). We also have [a blog post](https://www.sparkpost.com/blog/mandrill-alternative-sparkpost-survival-guide/#.Vty1MpMrJE4) that provides more detail.
